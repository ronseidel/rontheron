Four Messages

Shinier, stranger, and more emotional than his first effort — over-polished, undercooked, deeply unserious, and somehow still heartfelt. Thirteen tracks ranging from surreal multilingual collage to bittersweet breakup pop, closing with James Baldwin reminding us: "Your suffering is your bridge."

Mastered by Alberto De Icaza  ·  Mixed (tracks 6–8) by Hanan Rubenstein  ·  Mentors: David Greenbaum & Ed Fuhrman
